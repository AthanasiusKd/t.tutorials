FAQ

Thanos Tutorials is an online tutorial service that brings tutors and students together on a platform so as to give the tutors a wider reach while giving the students the option of picking from a large sample of tutors the one they think would be the most impactful on them

Once logged in, you can find a course in two ways. You can either search for a tutor you know has a class on that course and chose accordingly or you can search for the course and chose the tutor of your choice from the list of various tutors who take that course that appear.    

The tutors resulting from the search engine can be sorted by views, by cost of the course or by student ratings. All courses also have a preview of the course with the first video usually free to view to help you see the teaching method and course overview and thus make a sound decision.
